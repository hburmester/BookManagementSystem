Welcome to the Book Management System

About

This is a simple Book Management System. It allows you to perform various operations on books such as creating, updating, deleting, and listing books. Additionally, there is an endpoint to get the average price of books published after a specific year.

Endpoints

  • Create a new book: POST /books/
  • List all books: GET /books/
  • Get a single book by title: GET /books/{title}
  • Update a book: PUT /books/{title}
  • Delete a book: DELETE /books/{title}
  • Get average price after a year: GET /books/average_price/{year}
